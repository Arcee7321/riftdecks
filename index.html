About RiftDecks

RiftDecks is a community-made tool for the Riftbound TCG, designed to help players browse cards, build decks, and track their entire collection with ease.

★ Full Card Database

Search and browse every card in the game with detailed stats.

★ Advanced Deck Builder

Create decks with filtering, analysis, and easy editing.

★ Collection Tracker

Mark owned cards and find what you’re missing.
